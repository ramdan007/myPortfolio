02

PASSION

  • DESIGN

    I think design is like a "magic wand". If a service or product is the magic to solve a problem, it is the role of design to act as an intermediary for the user to master it. We strive every day to create a "magic wand" that matches the user.

  • TECHNOLOGY

    Technology has the power to change the lives of the world and individuals, as the phrase "well-developed science and technology is indistinguishable from magic." I want to be a person who can always catch up with the latest technology and respond to changes. I think it is the mission of designers as well as engineers to promote innovation.

  • STORY

    I love story-like content such as novels, movies, and manga. Stories can get people's attention and make things easier to understand and remember. I believe that if you can study every story pattern and give your output a story, you will get a big return.
